Our Story

Built by people who take road travel personally.

City Land Travels started in 2009 with three coaches and a simple belief: the hours between two cities shouldn't feel wasted or unsafe. Today we run over 100 Volvo and Scania coaches across 40+ routes, but the promise hasn't changed — every seat is tracked, every driver is vetted, and every journey is treated like it's carrying someone's family.

  • GPS-tracked fleet — know exactly where your bus is, always.
  • Verified drivers — background-checked, trained, and rested.
  • Hotel-grade cleaning — every coach sanitized before departure.
